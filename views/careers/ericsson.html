Ericsson Integration & Installation Field Engineer

EFI Integration Technician (Engineer, Furnish and Install)
Introduction:

Volks Resources is currently looking for an EF&I Integration Technician Nationwide This role requires site integration, training of in-house integrators. Qualified candidates preferred to have Ericsson EF&I Certification, 3 plus years of experience with integration work, antenna & line experience very helpful.

Responsibilities:
As part of site Commissioning and Integration of UMTS and LTE base stations, carriers require the following skills and experience:
  • Receive assignments and deploy to cell site locations to install, commission and integrate Ericsson UMTS & LTE equipment.
  • New Site Builds (NSB), Carrier Ads as well as Indoor systems including all necessary elements; installation, integration, testing, transport, alarm, power, ground, SIAD, functional and E911 call testing.
  • Using laptop and specialized software, load software and scripts in order to perform configuration as directed by Remote Integration Center.
  • May be required to provide support to troubleshoot system upon request of the Remote Integration Center.
  • Responsible to pick-up OEM equipment from customer or company warehouse location and care for, scan and report on equipment disposition.
  • Complete and deliver site completion report within deadline.
  • Complete and deliver site completion report within deadline.
  • Commissioning and integration of Smart Integrated Access Devices (SIAD) and Optical Baseband Interfaces (OBIF).
  • Experience with testing fiber and Coax such as sweep testing, OTDR and PIM testing.
  • Commissioning and integration of Cell Site Routers and Switches (Cisco, ALU, Ericsson).
  • Troubleshoot issues from the edge to the core of a network.
  • Ability to read, identify and change site configurations according to plans or drawings.
  • Upgrade BTS equipment to current Software levels and Install licenses, create and load scripts using BTS management software.
  • Provide support to switch and field technicians during installation and integration.
  • Assist in the development of MOP’s and procedures for commissioning, integrating and testing.
  • Perform E911 and Acceptance call testing and alarm clearing
  • Perform Parameter Audit.
  • Works aggressively on multiple job tasks to help meet timelines.
  • Ability to thoroughly document, report and communicate processes, procedures, and results.
  • Preparing Close Out Packages (COP)
  • Produce a punch list and coordinate completion of punch list items.
